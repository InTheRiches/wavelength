Below are starter plans to help kickstart your journey. Remember, these serve as a guide and might not fit everyone perfectly. Feel free to adjust and personalize based on your preferences, fitness levels, and goals.

These plans do include a rep and set count, but they are currently setup for powerlifting goals, and you should configure it to meet your specifics goals. If you are curious what is best for you, see [Reps and Sets](/muscles/training/basics/reps-and-sets).

# PPL Intermediate / Advanced plan
Run this as Legs, Push, Pull, Legs 2, Push 2, Pull 2, Rest, Repeat. You can have an optional rest day between pull 1 and legs 2.
