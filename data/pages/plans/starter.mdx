Welcome to the world of weightlifting! Below are starter plans to help kickstart your journey. Remember, these serve as a guide and might not fit everyone perfectly. Feel free to adjust and personalize based on your preferences, fitness levels, and goals.

These plans don't include set or rep counts, as those differ based off your specific goals. If you are curious what is best for you, see [Reps and Sets](/muscles/training/basics/reps-and-sets).

# PPL Intermediate / Advanced plan

Run this as Legs, Push, Pull, Legs 2, Push 2, Pull 2, Rest, Repeat. You can have an optional rest day between pull 1 and legs 2.
